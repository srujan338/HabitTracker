# Habit.Space - Professional Operations Manual 📘

Welcome to the official manual for **Habit.Space**. This document is designed for users who demand a high-performance system for personal growth and habit optimization.

## 📐 System Overview

Habit.Space operates on the principle of **Atomic Habits** integrated with **RPG Mechanics**. Your journey is quantified through data-driven metrics and visual rewards.

### 1. The XP & Levelling Algorithm
Your progress is tracked via Experience Points (XP). The system rewards consistency over intensity.
- **Base Completion**: `+10 XP` per habit check-in.
- **Momentum Bonus**: `+5 XP` for maintaining a streak.
- **Creation Reward**: `+25 XP` for architecting a new habit.
- **Event Mastery**: Variable XP rewards for completing time-bound challenges.

### 2. Trait Analytics (Hex-Stats)
The system calculates six core attributes derived from your historical data:
- 💪 **Discipline**: Evaluated based on high-priority task completion.
- 🔄 **Consistency**: Measures the standard deviation of your check-in intervals.
- 🎯 **Dedication**: Total volume of successful habit completions.
- 🧠 **Focus**: Analyzes the depth of engagement in learning-type habits.
- 🎨 **Creativity**: Tracks participation in divergent-thinking activities.
- 🛡️ **Resilience**: A "recovery" metric measuring the speed of streak restoration after a lapse.

## 🎮 Interface Navigation

### **The Command Center (Dashboard)**
Your primary interface for daily operations. 
- **Check-in**: Immediate data entry for habit completion.
- **Analytics Hover**: Detailed metrics available for every active habit.
- **XP Bar**: Real-time visual feedback on your level progression.

### **The Laboratory (My Habits)**
Where habits are engineered. 
- **Categorization**: Use predefined tags (Health, Finance, Productivity) for better trait mapping.
- **Data Export**: Professional CSV export functionality for external data analysis.

### **The Companion Chamber (Pet Room)**
An emotional feedback loop. Your **Arcane Companion** reacts to your data, providing motivational context and visual evolution based on your level.

## 🛠️ Advanced Workflows

### Habit Engineering
To create a high-impact habit:
1. Identify a target area (e.g., *Deep Work*).
2. Select the **Productivity** category to boost your **Discipline** stat.
3. Use a clear, action-oriented name (e.g., "*60m Focused Coding*").

### Handling "Lapses"
The system is designed with **Resilience** in mind. A broken streak is not a failure but a data point. Use the **Resilience** stat to monitor how quickly you return to baseline consistency.
